micro:bit AI Connect 🤖

Give your micro:bit project AI superpowers! This site lets your micro:bit use your computer's webcam or microphone to recognize images, poses, and sounds based on your own trained AI model.

Follow the steps below — train your model, load the starter code onto your micro:bit, then click Connect to bring it all together!

1.  Go to Google Teachable Machine

A browser popup will ask you to select a serial port.
It may appear as mbed Serial Port or USB Serial Device.
